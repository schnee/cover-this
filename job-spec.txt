Director of Data Science, Risk

Global Risk Decision Science and Data Capabilities is a key team in Risk & Compliance organization that ensures the company’s products and processes deliver robust fraud and credit decisions while providing a great customer experience and supporting the company's growth. We are a team of data and analytics driven experts with an outstanding ability and sense of mission to find solutions through innovation and collaboration.

We are looking for an accomplished leader as Director of Data Science, Risk, who will lead a team of data scientists and will be responsible for developing and managing the fraud and credit machine learning models leading to best-in-class risk decision-making. The successful candidate will have deep knowledge and experience in data science, preferably in commercial and small business payments. The successful candidate will be collaborative and a strong communicator with high EQ to work in a matrixed organization. This position will report directly to the VP of Global Risk Decision Science and Data Capabilities.

Roles And Responsibilities

Lead a team of data scientists and analysts to build machine learning models
Provide thought leadership in analytics for fraud and credit decisioning, and work together with Strategy and Operations teams to define, evaluate, and implement potential solutions
Leverage technology and analytical techniques to optimize decisions while considering implications to customer experience
Evaluate external opportunities (i.e. risk data providers, transaction monitoring solutions) to supplement internal capabilities with a firm understanding for tradeoffs in decisions for internal and external solutions
Define data opportunity roadmaps to improve delivery gains against key performance indicators
Collaborate with key stakeholders and cross-functional partners across the company including Risk Operations, Technology, Bank, Marketing, Finance, Legal, Risk and Compliance/BSA/AML.

Experience And Abilities

Bachelor’s degree required, advanced degree strongly preferred
10+ years of experience leading an data or analytics team
Evidence of working across analytics and engineering organizations to deliver on collaborative project goals
Experience with analytics and data lifecycle management
Understanding of analytics and engineering data ecosystems
Ability to think critically in order to analyze problems and develop creative, practical and efficient solutions for a multinational company
Strong communications skills that distill complex data and systems issues to both technical and non-technical audiences
Strong people leadership and collaboration skills with enterprise-first mindset
Leadership qualities that motivate collaboration with others, guiding the prioritization of initiatives
Understanding of project management methodologies and the tradeoffs between them
Payments industry experience a plus
